About italianConsegna
Tradition since 1999


italianConsegna is a Singapore tech success story. After moving from Singapore to Atlanta, our founder was surprised to find it was nearly impossible to get great quality food delivered. So he made it his personal mission to bring great restaurants closer to their customers. We now operate in over 100 towns and cities across the US, employing over 600 software engineers and employees in our US headquarters, working with more than 8,000 partner restaurants and engaging 15,000 riders.
